Our Mission
Our mission is to help people throughout the world who are suffering from poverty and hunger. We help the world's impoverished by feeding, empowering, clothing, and providing basic necessities including food, water, electricity, shelter, and healthcare.
Our Vision
We expect a community in which everyone has access to sufficient nutritious food.
WFP AND THE SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS
The 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development, which was endorsed by all UN Member States in 2015, is focused on achieving peace and prosperity for people and the planet. The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are 17 action points put out in the agenda to "free the human race from the tyranny of poverty" and "move the world onto a sustainable and resilient path" by 2030. Zero Hunger is the second Sustainable Development Goal. We are a key player in efforts to achieve SDG 2, which seeks to end hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture, by feeding over 100 million of the world's poorest people and assisting millions more around the world to improve their nutrition and build sustainable livelihoods.
