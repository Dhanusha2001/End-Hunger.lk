Seeds and Skills for Women to Grow Vegetables


We supply vegetable seeds and support to elderly women so they can grow
food! Women farmers produce more than half of the food produced in the
developing countries, despite owning less than 2% of the land and receiving
little help in the form of seeds, tools, and education. We join women's
gardening initiatives in some of the world's poorest
countries, such as Madagascar, Guatemala, Liberia,
and Kenya.
