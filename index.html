The globalization is exacerbating world hunger

According to UNICEF research, as globalization affects food production and distribution, new kinds of malnutrition emerge, endangering the health of billions of people. As a result of this transition, there is an increase in undernutrition, concealed hunger, and overweight people.

811 million

The number of undernourished people reached 811 million in 2021.

62%

In 2021 Asia accounted for nearly two thirds, 62 percent, of the world’s hungry.
