Global hunger continues to rise, says UN report......

New York, 15 July 2019 - In 2018, an estimated 820 million people went hungry, up from 811 million the year before, marking the third year in a row of increases. According to a new version of the annual The State of Food Security and Nutrition in the World study issued today, this highlights the enormous difficulty of reaching the Sustainable Development Goal of Zero Hunger by 2030.
